Biography

Image

Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, known professionally as Lana Del Rey, is an American singer, songwriter and record producer. Her music is noted for its stylized cinematic quality; its themes of tragic romance, glamour, and melancholia; and its references to pop culture, particularly 1950s and 1960s Americana.
